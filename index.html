About Us

Phonix Fold Interlining traces its origins to a family-run apparel business that began over four decades ago. Today, Phonix Fold stands as one of the leading suppliers of woven and non-woven interlining in South Asia and the Middle East. Over the years, we have established a robust network of loyal clients across three continents, where our distinctive logo symbolizes exceptional quality and reliability. We collaborate with cutting-edge manufacturing facilities worldwide, ensuring complete control over the production processâ€”from yarn selection to fabric weaving to the final finishing and coating of our interlining products. Each interlining product is crafted according to our proprietary standards. At Phonix Fold, we believe in delivering customized solutions rather than off-the-shelf products. The result? Premium interlining that consistently exceeds the expectations of garment manufacturers globally.
