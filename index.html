About Us

At PHOENIXFORD Interlining, we are committed to transforming the textile industry with premium interlining solutions that ensure quality, durability, and innovation. Our mission is to empower manufacturers and designers with materials that add structure, comfort, and value to their creations. Founded with a vision for precision and reliability, PHOENIXFORD Interlining specializes in providing a wide range of woven, non-woven, and fusible interlining materials. These solutions cater to industries such as fashion, home furnishings, and industrial textiles, ensuring every product meets the highest standards of performance and sustainability. Our team of skilled professionals combines technical expertise with a passion for quality, ensuring meticulous attention to detail at every step. We prioritize innovation by leveraging advanced technologies to meet the evolving demands of the textile sector. At PHOENIXFORD, we pride ourselves on a customer-centric approach, offering tailored solutions and on-time delivery to foster long-lasting partnerships. Sustainability is at the core of our operations, as we strive to minimize environmental impact through eco-friendly practices and materials. Our vision is to lead the global interlining industry by inspiring excellence, driving innovation, and building trust with our clients. With PHOENIXFORD Interlining, you can expect products and services that redefine the standards of quality and reliability in textiles. ❤️
